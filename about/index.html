About

Who I Am

I am a thirteen year old hobbyist web developer / high school student highly interested in computer science and information technology. Most of the time, when I'm not at school, I browse the Internet and develop websites and web applications. I don't believe in using templates or "cheating" to get ahead - when I do something, I tend to do it from scratch. I fuss over projects, making sure they not only meet their standards, but mine as well. I am very excited for the future of both myself and technology in general.
