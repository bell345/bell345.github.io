About

Me

I am a fifteen year old high school student who likes to program and make websites in my spare time. I am highly interested in computer science and technology. I am very keen to learn about technological subjects and to expand my knowledge. When I'm not programming, I'm on Reddit and playing games on Steam. Personally, I am greatly excited about the future of computer science and technology — it's a better time than ever to get into the industry!

I own two computers, one I built myself (i3-4160 and GTX 750 Ti if anyone's asking) and one for school. I'm a big Linux nut, and I pray for the day that it finally becomes accepted enough that I'll be able to play all my games on it, no problems. I like listening to Spotify, especially alternative rock and electronic music. Say what you will about my music tastes. I'm adaptable with programming languages, but I'm most comfortable with JavaScript, C++ and the like.

This Website

It started out as a side project after I had finished working on a website assignment for school. I would make quick little applications and put it on this website on a local web server. It only got off the ground and into the cloud once I moved to GitHub Pages. It's still nothing that remarkable, but I'd like to think that one day it'll be one of those things you can look back on when I'm all grown up.

Contacting Me

I do have Facebook, Twitter, Skype, all that, but I'd rather not share to people whom I don't necessarily know anything about.
